01 ABOUT

I’m Ashar Zarrar, a software engineer from Howard Beach, Queens. Growing up by the water helped me stay curious and reflective. Before joining The Marcy Lab School, I planned to take the traditional college route, but instead chose to take a gap year and begin my fellowship first.

I’ve always been interested in computer engineering and hardware. Over time, I realized that what truly brings machines to life is software. That curiosity pushed me into software engineering and coding, even though I started with very little experience.

I’m inspired by building full-scale applications while understanding what’s happening behind the scenes. I enjoy UI design, debugging, and blending creativity with logic.

I bring a unique perspective from computer engineering, with a strong work ethic and emphasis on communication, teamwork, and continuous growth.
